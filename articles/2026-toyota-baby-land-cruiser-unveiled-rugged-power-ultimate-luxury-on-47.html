At the same time, its luxurious interior features like heated seats and a moonroof provide a comfortable and enjoyable driving experience.

According to Toyota's Chief Engineer, "The Baby Land Cruiser is designed to be a versatile vehicle that can handle a variety of driving conditions, from city streets to off-road trails.
