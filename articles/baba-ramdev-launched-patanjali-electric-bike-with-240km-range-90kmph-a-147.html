Patanjali, a well-known Indian conglomerate, has ventured into the electric vehicle market with the launch of its new electric bike. The company, founded by Baba Ramdev, aims to provide an affordable and eco-friendly transportation solution for the middle class. The new electric bike boasts an impressive range of 240km on a single charge and can reach speeds of up to 90kmph.

The launch of the electric bike is a significant step for Patanjali, as it marks the company's entry into the rapidly growing electric vehicle market in India. With the government's push for electric mobility and the increasing demand for environmentally friendly transportation options, Patanjali's electric bike is well-positioned to capture a significant share of the market. The bike's budget-friendly price point and impressive features make it an attractive option for the middle class, who are looking for affordable and reliable transportation solutions.

The bike also features a regenerative braking system, which helps to recharge the battery and improve the overall efficiency of the vehicle.

In terms of design, the Patanjali electric bike has a sleek and modern look, with a lightweight and durable frame. The bike also features a comfortable seating arrangement and a spacious storage compartment, making it ideal for daily commuting. The bike's safety features include a robust braking system and a stable suspension, which provide a smooth and comfortable ride.

The Patanjali electric bike is also equipped with a range of advanced technologies, including a digital instrument cluster and a mobile app that allows users to track their vehicle's performance and location.
