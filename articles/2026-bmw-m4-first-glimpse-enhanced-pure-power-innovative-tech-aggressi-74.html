What's New in the 2026 BMW M4?

The 2026 BMW M4 boasts an array of innovative features, including a 3.0-liter inline-6 engine that produces 473 horsepower and 406 lb-ft of torque. This powerful engine is paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission and rear-wheel drive, allowing for seamless acceleration and precise handling. The vehicle's adaptive suspension and dynamic stability control work in tandem to provide a smooth ride, even on the most challenging roads.

Inside the 2026 BMW M4, you'll find a host of cutting-edge technologies, including a 12.3-inch infotainment screen, Apple CarPlay, and Android Auto. The vehicle also features a 16-speaker Harman Kardon sound system, providing an immersive audio experience. With its 14-way power-adjustable seats and heated steering wheel, the 2026 BMW M4 is the epitome of comfort and convenience.

In terms of safety, the 2026 BMW M4 has got you covered. The vehicle features a 5-star overall safety rating and comes equipped with a range of advanced safety features, including forward collision warning, automatic emergency braking, and lane departure warning.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the top speed of the 2026 BMW M4?

The 2026 BMW M4 has a top speed of 155 mph, although this can be increased to 180 mph with the optional M Driver's Package. The vehicle's 0-60 mph time is just 3.9 seconds, making it one of the fastest vehicles in its class.

Does the 2026 BMW M4 come with a manual transmission?

Yes, the 2026 BMW M4 is available with a 6-speed manual transmission, in addition to the standard 8-speed automatic transmission. The manual transmission is a popular choice among driving enthusiasts, as it provides a more engaging and immersive driving experience.

What safety features does the 2026 BMW M4 offer?

The 2026 BMW M4 features a range of advanced safety features, including forward collision warning, automatic emergency braking, and lane departure warning. The vehicle also has a 5-star overall safety rating and comes equipped with eight airbags, providing optimal protection for drivers and passengers.

Can I customize the 2026 BMW M4 to my liking?

Yes, the 2026 BMW M4 offers a wide range of customization options, including exterior paint colors, interior trim materials, and wheel designs. With its BMW Individual program, you can create a truly unique vehicle that reflects your personal style and preferences.
