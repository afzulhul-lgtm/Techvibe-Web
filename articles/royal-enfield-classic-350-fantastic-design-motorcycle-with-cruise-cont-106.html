Royal Enfield Classic 350 fantastic design motorcycle with cruise control features, price is affordable

By Sarah Mitchell
Published: February 28, 2026 Updated: February 28, 2026 5 Min Read

The 2026 Royal Enfield Classic 350 is a motorcycle that embodies the perfect blend of vintage design and modern technology. With its stunning Emerald shade and Commando Sand color options, this bike is sure to turn heads on the road. The Bobber-inspired twist and floating single seat give it a unique look, while the mini ape-hanger bars and white-walled tubeless tires add to its classic charm.

Introduction to the Royal Enfield Classic 350

The Royal Enfield Classic 350 is powered by a 349cc J-series engine, producing 20.2 bhp and 27 Nm torque. The single-cylinder thump of the engine is music to the ears of any bike enthusiast. The five-speed gearbox and assist and slipper clutch make for smooth shifting, while the dual-channel ABS ensures safe braking.

The bike also features LED lighting, a USB Type-C port, and a top speed of 120 kmph. The telescopic fork and twin shocks provide a comfortable ride, while the fuel efficiency of 41 kmpl makes it a great option for touring.

Design and Features of the Royal Enfield Classic 350

The Royal Enfield Classic 350 has a manageable weight, making it easy to handle for women riders and new bikers. The bike also features a tripper navigation system, which provides turn-by-turn directions and makes it easy to navigate through unknown territories.

The classic design of the bike is sure to appeal to fans of the brand, while the modern features make it a great option for those looking for a reliable and efficient ride. The bike is available in several color options, including the stunning Emerald shade and Commando Sand.

Performance and Ergonomics of the Royal Enfield Classic 350

The Royal Enfield Classic 350 has a powerful engine that provides a smooth and comfortable ride. The bike has a low-end torque that makes it easy to navigate through city traffic, while the high-speed stability makes it a great option for touring.

The bike also features a pillion seat, making it a great option for those who want to ride with a passenger. The ergonomics of the bike are designed to provide a comfortable ride for both the rider and the pillion passenger.
