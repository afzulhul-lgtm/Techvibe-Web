Apple Shocks With Cheapest MacBook Ever at £599 – Launches Days After Bargain iPhone 17e

By Sarah Mitchell Senior Tech Editor
Published: April 28, 2026 Updated: April 28, 2026 6 Min Read

Apple has made a bold move in the tech industry by introducing its cheapest MacBook ever, with a price tag of £599. This unexpected announcement comes on the heels of the company's recent launch of the iPhone 17e, a more affordable version of its flagship smartphone. The new MacBook is set to disrupt the market with its unprecedented pricing, making it an attractive option for students, budget-conscious consumers, and businesses looking to upgrade their fleets.

The introduction of the £599 MacBook is a strategic move by Apple to expand its customer base and increase its market share. By offering a more affordable option, Apple aims to attract price-sensitive buyers who may have previously been deterred by the company's premium pricing. This shift in strategy is likely to have a significant impact on the tech industry, as it will put pressure on competitors to revisit their pricing models and offer more competitive options.

The New MacBook: Features and Specifications

The new MacBook boasts an impressive array of features, including a sleek design, fast processor, and ample storage. While the exact specifications have not been fully disclosed, it is expected to come with a range of configurations to cater to different user needs. The laptop's display is expected to be of high quality, with vibrant colors and excellent viewing angles, making it ideal for entertainment, productivity, and creative tasks.

One of the key highlights of the new MacBook is its portability. Weighing in at just a few pounds, it is perfect for students, freelancers, and business professionals who need a reliable and powerful laptop on the go. The device is also expected to have a long-lasting battery, allowing users to work, play, and stay connected for extended periods without needing to recharge.

Apple's decision to launch a more affordable MacBook is a nod to the changing needs of consumers. With the rise of remote work, online learning, and digital entertainment, there is a growing demand for affordable, high-quality laptops that can keep up with the demands of modern life. The new MacBook is poised to fill this gap, offering an attractive combination of performance, portability, and affordability.

Market Impact and Competition

The launch of the £599 MacBook is likely to send shockwaves through the tech industry, as competitors scramble to respond to Apple's aggressive pricing. The move is expected to put pressure on other manufacturers to revisit their pricing strategies and offer more competitive options. This could lead to a price war, with consumers ultimately benefiting from more affordable and feature-rich devices.

The new MacBook will face stiff competition from established players in the budget laptop market. However, Apple's strong brand reputation, ecosystem, and customer loyalty are likely to give it an edge. The company's ability to integrate its devices seamlessly, offering a cohesive user experience across different products, is a major draw for many consumers.
