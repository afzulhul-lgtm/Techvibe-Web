What's New in the Hyundai Tucson 2026?

So, what can you expect from the Hyundai Tucson 2026? For starters, the new model features a redesigned exterior with a more aggressive front grille and sleeker lines. The interior has also been revamped, with a new dashboard design and updated infotainment system. But the real star of the show is the hybrid powertrain, which combines a 1.6-liter turbocharged engine with an electric motor to produce 258 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque.

According to Hyundai officials, the new model is designed to provide a more comfortable and connected driving experience. With features like heated and ventilated seats, a premium audio system, and wireless charging, it's clear that the manufacturer has put a lot of thought into creating a vehicle that meets the needs of modern drivers. And with a starting price of $28,350, the Hyundai Tucson 2026 is an attractive option for those in the market for a new SUV.

Key Features of the Hyundai Tucson 2026

Some of the key features of the Hyundai Tucson 2026 include:

  • Hybrid powertrain with a 1.6-liter turbocharged engine and an electric motor
  • 10.25-inch touchscreen display with navigation and Apple CarPlay
  • Forward collision avoidance and lane departure warning
  • Heated and ventilated seats with premium leather trim
