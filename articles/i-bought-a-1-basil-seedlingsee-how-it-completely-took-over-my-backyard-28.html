Introduction to Basil Seedlings

Basil is one of the most popular herbs used in cooking, and it's easy to see why. This versatile plant is not only delicious, but it's also relatively easy to grow and maintain. With the right conditions and care, a basil seedling can grow into a thriving plant, producing an abundance of fresh leaves for months to come. Whether you're a seasoned gardener or just starting out, a basil seedling is a great addition to any backyard garden.

So, how did my $1 basil seedling turn into a giant basil plant garden? It all started with some viral gardening hacks that I discovered online.
