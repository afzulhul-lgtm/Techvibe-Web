The car's luxury design features a stylish exterior and a spacious interior, complete with premium leather seats and wood trim.

But what really sets the Toyota Century 2026 apart is its high-performance capabilities. The car's 4.6-liter V8 engine produces 460 horsepower and 398 lb-ft of torque, making it capable of going from 0-60mph in just 5.5 seconds.
