What is the Ford Hennessey Velociraptor?

The Ford Hennessey Velociraptor is a high-performance pickup truck that combines the best of both worlds - the ruggedness of a pickup truck and the power of a sports car. With its 6.2-liter supercharged V8 engine, this truck can go from 0-60 mph in just 4.5 seconds. But it's not just about speed; the Ford Hennessey Velociraptor also boasts a towing capacity of up to 8,000 pounds and a payload capacity of up to 1,500 pounds.

One of the most impressive features of the Ford Hennessey Velociraptor is its advanced suspension system, which provides a smooth ride even on the most rugged terrain. The truck also features a 12-inch touchscreen display with navigation, Apple CarPlay, and Android Auto, making it the perfect vehicle for both work and play.

Key Features

  • 750 horsepower engine
  • 3.0-liter twin-turbo powerhouse
  • 6.2-liter supercharged V8 engine
  • 0-60 mph in 4.5 seconds
  • Towing capacity of up to 8,000 pounds
  • Payload capacity of up to 1,500 pounds
