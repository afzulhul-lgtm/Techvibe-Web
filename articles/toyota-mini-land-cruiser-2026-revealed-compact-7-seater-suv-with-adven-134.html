What Makes the Toyota Mini Land Cruiser 2026 Special?

The Toyota Mini Land Cruiser 2026 boasts an impressive array of features, including a 2.5L 4-cylinder engine producing 203 horsepower and 184 lb-ft of torque. This engine is paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission and comes with a choice of front-wheel drive or all-wheel drive. The vehicle also features a 10.1-inch touchscreen display with Apple CarPlay and Android Auto, as well as a premium JBL sound system with 11 speakers.

In terms of safety, the Toyota Mini Land Cruiser 2026 comes equipped with a range of advanced features, including Toyota's Safety Sense 2.0 suite, which includes features like adaptive cruise control, lane departure warning, and automatic emergency braking. The vehicle also features a 5-star overall safety rating from the National Highway Traffic Safety Administration (NHTSA).
