Maruti Suzuki Dzire small size sedan comes with comfortable features , mileage is high

By Sarah Mitchell
Published: March 04, 2026 Updated: March 04, 2026 4 Min Read

Introduction to the Maruti Suzuki Dzire

The Maruti Suzuki Dzire is a small size sedan that comes with comfortable features and high mileage, making it a top choice for Indian car buyers. With its latest iteration, Maruti Suzuki has once again proven why it's a leader in the Indian automotive market. Priced from ₹6.26 lakh to ₹9.31 lakh (ex-showroom), the 2026 Dzire offers a perfect mix of affordability, efficiency, and family-friendly vibes.

Key Takeaways

* High mileage: up to 24.79kmpl for MT petrol and 25.71kmpl for AMT * Comfortable features: electric sunroof, automatic climate control, and rear AC vents * Modern tech: 9-inch SmartPlay Pro+ infotainment system with wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto * Safety features: 5-star Bharat NCAP and Global NCAP ratings, six airbags, and ESP

A Fresh Face That Turns Heads

The new Dzire has a sleek and premium design, with LED crystal vision headlamps, a bold grille, and two-tone precision-cut alloy wheels. The side profile boasts a shark-fin antenna, while the rear features 3D trinity LED lamps. The electric sunroof on higher trims lets city light pour in, making those Mumbai monsoons feel less gloomy.

Cabin Comfort Meets Modern Tech

The dual-tone sophisticated interiors of the Dzire hit you with elegance you don’t expect under ₹10 lakh. The floating 9-inch SmartPlay Pro+ infotainment system blasts immersive Surround Sense audio by ARKAMYS, with wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto keeping you connected without cables snagging your chai spill. Automatic climate control and rear AC vents ensure no one’s sweating in the back during Delhi summers.

Power and Efficiency That Won’t Break the Bank

The familiar 1.2-litre Z12E three-cylinder petrol churns 80bhp and 111.7Nm, paired with a slick 5-speed manual or AMT auto. ARAI claims 24.79kmpl for MT petrol, 25.71kmpl for AMT, and a stellar 33.73km/kg for CNG variants. Owners report 19-22kmpl mixed, 14-17 in cities, which crushes rivals like the Aura.
Variant Mileage (kmpl)
MT Petrol 24.79
AMT 25.71
CNG 33.73
“The Maruti Suzuki Dzire is a game-changer in the Indian automotive market, offering a perfect blend of style, comfort, and efficiency.” - R.C. Bhargava, Chairman, Maruti Suzuki India

Safety First, Without the Fuss

Maruti finally gets serious about crashes: 5-star Bharat NCAP and Global NCAP ratings across the board, even on base LXi. Six airbags standard, ESP, hill-hold, ABS with EBD, and ISOFIX anchors make it a fortress. Higher ZXi+ adds a 360-degree camera, TPMS, and reverse sensors.

Standing Tall Against Rivals

In the sub-4m ring, Dzire punches above: Hyundai Aura’s efficient but cramped; Honda Amaze comfy yet thirsty; Tata Tigor safe but bland. Dzire wins on value—better mileage, resale (hits 30 lakh units!), and network.
