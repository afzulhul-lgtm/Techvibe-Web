The vehicle's interior will also be highly customizable, allowing owners to personalize their V16 to suit their individual tastes and preferences. This level of flexibility will be a major draw for prospective buyers who value exclusivity and want their vehicle to reflect their unique personality.

The technology underpinning the 2026 Cadillac V16 will be equally impressive, with a range of advanced systems working in harmony to deliver exceptional performance, efficiency, and convenience.
