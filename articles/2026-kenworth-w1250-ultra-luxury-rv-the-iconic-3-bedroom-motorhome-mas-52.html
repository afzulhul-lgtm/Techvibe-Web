The 2026 Kenworth W1250 Ultra-Luxury RV is a sight to behold, with its custom-built design and powerful Paccar MX-13 engine producing between 405 and 510 horsepower and up to 1,850 lb-ft of torque. And, when paired with an Allison automatic transmission, this motorhome is a force to be reckoned with. But why does this matter? Well, for those who demand the best, this RV is a game-changer.

On the flip side, the exterior length typically reaches 45 feet, making it a substantial vehicle. Yet, with air-ride suspension, the ride is smooth and comparable to luxury coaches from Prevost and Marathon Coach.
