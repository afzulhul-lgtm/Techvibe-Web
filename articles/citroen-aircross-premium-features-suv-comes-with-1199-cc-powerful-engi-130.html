Citroen Aircross premium features SUV comes with 1199 cc powerful engine, check details

By Mike Reynolds Senior Editor
Published: May 06, 2026 Updated: May 06, 2026 6 Min Read

The automotive market is witnessing a significant surge in the demand for compact SUVs, and the Citroen Aircross is one of the latest entrants in this segment. With its sleek design, spacious interior, and powerful engine, the Aircross is poised to give its competitors a run for their money. Estimates suggest that the Aircross will be priced competitively, starting from approximately $25,000, making it an attractive option for buyers looking for a feature-packed SUV without breaking the bank.

The Citroen Aircross is built on the PSA Group's EMP1 platform, which also underpins other models like the Peugeot 3008 and the Opel Grandland X. This platform provides a solid foundation for the Aircross, allowing it to offer a smooth ride, responsive handling, and a comfortable driving experience. The Aircross is expected to be available in various trim levels, including a base model, a mid-range variant, and a top-of-the-line version with all the bells and whistles.

Key Specs and Features Breakdown

The Citroen Aircross is powered by a 1.2-liter, three-cylinder turbocharged engine that produces an estimated 130 horsepower and 230 Nm of torque. This engine is paired with a six-speed manual transmission or an eight-speed automatic transmission, depending on the trim level. The Aircross also features a range of advanced safety features, including lane departure warning, blind spot detection, and automatic emergency braking. Other notable features include an 8-inch touchscreen infotainment system, Apple CarPlay and Android Auto connectivity, and a wireless charging pad.

In terms of design, the Aircross boasts a sleek and modern exterior with a distinctive front grille, LED headlights, and a sloping roofline. The interior is equally impressive, with a spacious cabin, comfortable seats, and a range of premium materials. The Aircross also offers a generous cargo area, with a capacity of up to 520 liters, making it an ideal vehicle for road trips and family vacations.

Some of the key features of the Citroen Aircross include:

  • 1.2-liter turbocharged engine with estimated 130 horsepower and 230 Nm of torque
  • Six-speed manual or eight-speed automatic transmission
  • Advanced safety features, including lane departure warning and automatic emergency braking
  • 8-inch touchscreen infotainment system with Apple CarPlay and Android Auto connectivity
  • Wireless charging pad and premium materials throughout the cabin

How It Compares to the Previous Model

The Citroen Aircross is a significant improvement over its predecessor, the C3 Aircross. The new model features a more modern design, a more powerful engine, and a range of advanced safety features. The Aircross also offers a more spacious interior, with increased legroom and cargo capacity. In comparison to its competitors, the Aircross is priced competitively and offers a unique blend of style, comfort, and features.

The Aircross is expected to compete with other compact SUVs like the Nissan Qashqai, the Hyundai Tucson, and the Volkswagen Tiguan. While these models have their strengths, the Aircross is poised to stand out with its distinctive design, comfortable ride, and generous features list. Estimates suggest that the Aircross will be a top contender in its segment, with sales expected to be strong in the first year.

Performance — What Tests and Users Show

Initial tests and user reviews suggest that the Citroen Aircross is a capable and comfortable vehicle. The 1.2-liter turbocharged engine provides ample power for city driving and highway cruising, while the six-speed manual transmission is smooth and responsive. The Aircross also handles well, with a comfortable ride and responsive steering.

In terms of fuel economy, the Aircross is estimated to deliver up to 45 mpg in the city and 55 mpg on the highway, making it a fuel-efficient option for daily commuting. The Aircross also features a range of driving modes, including Eco, Normal, and Sport, which allow drivers to customize their driving experience.

Price, Availability, and Where to Buy

The Citroen Aircross is expected to be available in various trim levels, with prices starting from approximately $25,000 for the base model. The mid-range variant is expected to start at around $28,000, while the top-of-the-line model will likely be priced at around $32,000. The Aircross will be available at Citroen dealerships and online, with test drives and purchases available soon.

Buyers can also expect a range of financing options and incentives, including low-interest loans and lease deals. The Aircross will also be available for purchase online, with a range of digital tools and services to facilitate the buying process.
