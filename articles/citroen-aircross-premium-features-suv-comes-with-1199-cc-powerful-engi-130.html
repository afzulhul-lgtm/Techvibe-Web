Citroen Aircross premium features SUV comes with 1199 cc powerful engine, check details

By Sarah Mitchell
Published: March 04, 2026 Updated: March 04, 2026 4 Min Read

The Citroen Aircross is turning heads in the Indian market with its refreshed design, unbeatable ride comfort, and value-packed pricing. As a compact SUV evolving from the popular C3 Aircross lineage, it's set to shake up the midsize segment.

With its bolder front fascia, sleek LED projector headlamps, and amped-up cladding around the wheel arches and bumpers, the Aircross grabs attention right away. Inside, the cabin feels more premium than before, featuring soft-touch materials, champagne trims, and power window switches conveniently located on the doors.

Citroen Aircross Premium Features

The Aircross offers a range of premium features, including rear AC vents, flexible seating that can switch between five or seven seats effortlessly, and automatic climate control. The power-folding ORVMs make parking in tight spots a breeze, while the 360-degree camera, cruise control, and ventilated front seats add to the overall comfort and convenience.

Stylish Refresh with SUV Swagger

The Aircross boasts a stylish refresh with SUV swagger, featuring a bold front fascia, sleek LED projector headlamps, and amped-up cladding around the wheel arches and bumpers. The interior feels more premium than before, with soft-touch materials, champagne trims, and power window switches conveniently located on the doors.

Citroen Aircross Powertrains

Under the hood, the Aircross offers two petrol options tailored for Indian roads. The entry-level 1.2-litre naturally aspirated engine churns out 82hp and 115Nm, paired exclusively with a five-speed manual. The turbocharged 1.2-litre PureTech unit offers 110hp and a torquey 205Nm on tap, available with a slick six-speed manual or a smooth six-speed torque converter automatic.

Safety First, Without Compromising Fun

The Aircross stands tall with its five-star Bharat NCAP rating for adult protection, earned through a high-strength steel body and over 40 active safety aids. Six airbags come standard across variants, along with ESP, hill-hold assist, TPMS, and ISOFIX mounts, making it one of the safest in its class.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is the price of Citroen Aircross?

The Citroen Aircross starts at Rs 8.29 lakh ex-showroom for the base You variant, going up to Rs 14.10 lakh for the top-end Max Turbo AT variant.

What are the safety features of Citroen Aircross?

The Aircross comes with a range of safety features, including six airbags, ESP, hill-hold assist, TPMS, and ISOFIX mounts, making it one of the safest in its class.

What is the fuel efficiency of Citroen Aircross?

The Aircross offers a claimed mileage of 18-19.5kmpl, with real-world tests showing it handles mixed conditions well, sipping fuel without compromising on pep.

What are the competitors of Citroen Aircross?

The Aircross competes with the Hyundai Creta, Kia Seltos, Tata Nexon, and Maruti Brezza in the midsize SUV segment.
