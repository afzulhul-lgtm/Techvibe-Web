2026 TVS Apache RTR 160 rocket engine motorcycle comes with sporty design

By Sarah Mitchell
Published: February 28, 2026 Updated: February 28, 2026 4 Min Read

Introduction to the 2026 TVS Apache RTR 160

The 2026 TVS Apache RTR 160 is set to revolutionize the Indian market with its aggressive stance, sculpted tank, and fang-like LED DRLs. This 160cc motorcycle is designed for those with a racing obsession, offering special editions in black and champagne gold schemes. With its muscular extensions and hydroformed handlebars, the Apache RTR 160 exudes a sense of power and style.

Key Features and Specifications

The 2026 TVS Apache RTR 160 comes equipped with a 159.7cc oil-cooled engine, featuring a 4-valve configuration that produces 17.55 PS of power at 9,250 rpm and 14.73 Nm of torque. The RT-Fi tech enables the rider to switch between Sport, Urban, and Rain modes, ensuring a smooth ride in various conditions. The Glide Through Technology reduces vibrations, while the double-cradle chassis and multi-body dynamics provide a stability boost.

The Apache RTR 160 boasts golden USD forks and a mono-shock rear, resulting in a weight of 143 kg. The dual-channel ABS, petal discs, and radial rear tires offer track-level confidence, while the 5-inch TFT screen provides Bluetooth connectivity, navigation, and voice assist. The race telemetry and class-D projector headlamp add to the bike's sporty appeal.

Engine and Performance

The 159.7cc engine is designed to deliver exceptional performance, with a top speed of 90-100 kmph and a fuel efficiency of 45 kmpl. The vibrations are minimal, ensuring a comfortable ride for both the rider and pillion riders. The rear suspension and sidestand are designed for convenience, while the fun factor is amplified by the bike's motorsport DNA.

Design and Features

The 2026 TVS Apache RTR 160 features a sporty design, with full LED lighting, connectivity, and safety features. The bullpup exhaust and traction control add to the bike's aggressive stance, while the uniform projector headlights provide excellent visibility. The speed, smarts, and soul of the Apache RTR 160 make it a compelling choice for those seeking a high-performance motorcycle.
