Understanding the Polar Vortex Disruption

The polar vortex disruption 2026 is a complex weather phenomenon that occurs when the polar vortex, a circular band of strong winds that surrounds the North Pole, becomes distorted and dips into the lower 48 states. This can bring extremely cold temperatures, heavy snowfall, and high winds to the affected areas, making travel and daily life extremely hazardous.

The polar vortex is a natural phenomenon that occurs when the jet stream, a fast-moving band of air that flows from west to east, becomes weakened and allows the polar vortex to dip into the lower 48 states. This can happen when there is a high-pressure system over the Arctic, which pushes the polar vortex southward.
