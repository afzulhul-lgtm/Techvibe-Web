Introduction to the 2026 Toyota Corolla Cross

The 2026 Toyota Corolla Cross is built on the same platform as the Corolla sedan, but with a higher ground clearance and a more rugged design. This gives the vehicle a sturdy appearance, while also providing improved visibility and a more commanding view of the road. The Corolla Cross also features a spacious interior, with plenty of room for passengers and cargo. The vehicle's hybrid powertrain is designed to provide excellent fuel economy, making it an attractive option for those who want to reduce their environmental impact.

One of the key features of the 2026 Toyota Corolla Cross is its advanced safety features. The vehicle comes equipped with a range of standard safety features, including lane departure warning, adaptive cruise control, and automatic emergency braking. These features are designed to help prevent accidents and protect occupants in the event of a collision. The Corolla Cross also features a range of convenient technologies, including a touchscreen infotainment system and a suite of connected services.

The 2026 Toyota Corolla Cross is available in a range of trim levels, each with its own unique features and options. Higher trim levels add additional features, such as a premium audio system, a power moonroof, and a range of exterior design elements.
