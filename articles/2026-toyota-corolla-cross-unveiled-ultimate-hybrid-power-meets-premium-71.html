What is the 2026 Toyota Corolla Cross, and What Are Its Key Features?

The 2026 Toyota Corolla Cross is a compact SUV that builds upon the success of the Corolla sedan, offering a more spacious and comfortable driving experience. With a 2.0-liter 4-cylinder engine and an electric motor, this vehicle produces a combined 194 horsepower and 176 lb-ft of torque. The 2026 Toyota Corolla Cross also features an 8-inch touchscreen display, Apple CarPlay and Android Auto compatibility, and a premium JBL audio system with 9 speakers.

In terms of safety, the 2026 Toyota Corolla Cross comes equipped with a suite of advanced features, including adaptive cruise control, lane departure warning, and blind spot monitoring. With a 5-star overall safety rating from the National Highway Traffic Safety Administration (NHTSA), this vehicle is designed to provide peace of mind for drivers and passengers alike.

Key Safety Features

  • Toyota Safety Sense 3.0 suite of advanced safety features
  • 8 airbags, including a driver's knee airbag and passenger seat cushion airbag
  • Rear cross traffic alert and blind spot monitoring
  • Electronic stability control and traction control
