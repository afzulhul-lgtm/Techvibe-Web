That's where it gets interesting. The existing Harley-Davidson Nightster and Sportster S already represent this direction, with the Sportster S producing 121 horsepower from its Revolution Max 1250T engine and featuring a radically different design language compared to traditional touring Harleys. A Viper Cruiser concept extension of this platform would likely feature an even more dramatically stretched and lowered body, premium materials including carbon fiber and machined aluminum detailing, and performance upgrades including upgraded suspension and braking.
