The Maruti Suzuki Fronx boasts a unique design that sets it apart from other compact SUVs in the market. The Fronx also features a range of advanced safety features, including multiple airbags, anti-lock braking system (ABS), and electronic stability program (ESP), which provide drivers with added peace of mind.

The interior of the Fronx is designed to be comfortable and convenient, with a range of features that enhance the driving experience. The vehicle features a premium infotainment system, a multifunction steering wheel, and a range of storage compartments that provide ample space for passengers and cargo. The Fronx also features a range of advanced technology features, including a touchscreen display, navigation system, and Bluetooth connectivity, which make it easy to stay connected on the go.

The Fronx is available in a range of trim levels, each with its own unique set of features and options. The base model features a range of standard features, including air conditioning, power windows, and a music system, while the higher-end models offer more advanced features, such as a sunroof, leather seats, and a premium sound system. With its range of trim levels and options, the Fronx is designed to cater to the needs of a wide range of drivers.

Performance and Safety

The Maruti Suzuki Fronx is powered by a 1.2L petrol engine, which provides a perfect balance of power and fuel efficiency. The engine produces a maximum power output of 89 horsepower and a maximum torque output of 113 Nm, making it suitable for both city driving and long road trips. The Fronx also features a range of advanced safety features, including multiple airbags, ABS, and ESP, which provide drivers with added peace of mind.

The Fronx has undergone rigorous testing and has received a range of safety certifications, including a 5-star rating from the Global New Car Assessment Program (GNCAP). The vehicle's safety features are designed to provide comprehensive protection for drivers and passengers, and its advanced technology features, such as a rearview camera and parking sensors, make it easy to maneuver in tight spaces.

The Fronx also features a range of advanced driver assistance systems, including a lane departure warning system and a blind spot monitoring system, which provide drivers with added confidence and control. With its range of safety features and advanced technology, the Fronx is designed to provide a safe and enjoyable driving experience.
