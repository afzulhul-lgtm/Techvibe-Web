What Makes the Maruti Suzuki FRONX Amazing?

The Maruti Suzuki FRONX's amazing design is more than just skin-deep. Under the hood, it boasts a 1.2L petrol engine that produces 89 horsepower and 113 Nm of torque, making it perfect for city driving and long road trips alike. The engine is paired with a 5-speed manual transmission or an optional AMT gearbox, providing a smooth and seamless shifting experience.

In terms of comfort features, the Maruti Suzuki FRONX has it all. The SUV comes with automatic climate control, rear AC vents, and heated seats, ensuring that you and your passengers stay comfortable no matter the weather. The 7-inch touchscreen infotainment system is another notable feature, offering seamless connectivity and navigation.

But what really sets the Maruti Suzuki FRONX apart is its focus on safety. The SUV comes with a range of advanced safety features, including dual airbags, ABS with EBD, and rear parking sensors, giving you peace of mind on the road.
