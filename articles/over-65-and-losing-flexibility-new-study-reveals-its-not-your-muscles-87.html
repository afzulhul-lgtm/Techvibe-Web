How Does the Nervous System Affect Flexibility?

The nervous system plays a crucial role in controlling our movements and maintaining our flexibility. As we age, the nerves that connect our brain to our muscles and joints can become damaged or less efficient, leading to reduced flexibility and increased stiffness. But by understanding how the nervous system affects flexibility, we can take steps to improve our range of motion and reduce the risk of age-related diseases.
