The IRS refunds are linked to several key tax credits, including the Earned Income Tax Credit (EITC) and the Child Tax Credit. The credits are refundable, meaning that if the credit exceeds the amount of taxes owed, the taxpayer will receive a refund for the difference.

The EITC, in particular, has undergone significant changes in recent years, with expansions to the eligibility criteria and increases to the credit amount.
