One of the key highlights of the 2026 Toyota Corolla Cross is its powerful and efficient engine. The vehicle is expected to come with a range of engine options, including a 1.8-liter petrol engine and a 1.8-liter hybrid engine. These engines will be paired with a variety of transmission options, including a continuously variable transmission (CVT) and a six-speed manual transmission.

In terms of safety features, the 2026 Toyota Corolla Cross is expected to come equipped with a range of advanced technologies. These include features such as adaptive cruise control, lane departure warning, and automatic emergency braking. The vehicle will also feature a comprehensive airbag system, including front, side, and curtain airbags.
