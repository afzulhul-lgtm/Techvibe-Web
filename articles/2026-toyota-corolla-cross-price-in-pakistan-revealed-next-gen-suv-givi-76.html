What's New in the 2026 Toyota Corolla Cross?

The 2026 Toyota Corolla Cross is a significant upgrade from its predecessor, featuring a more powerful 1.8L engine and a 7-speed CVT transmission. This combination provides a smooth and responsive driving experience, making it perfect for both city commutes and long road trips. Additionally, the SUV boasts a 360-degree camera system and adaptive cruise control, ensuring a safe and comfortable ride.

In terms of design, the 2026 Toyota Corolla Cross has a sleek and modern exterior, with a bold front grille and 18-inch alloy wheels. The interior is equally impressive, featuring a 9-inch touchscreen display and a premium audio system. With its spacious cabin and ample cargo space, this SUV is ideal for families and adventure-seekers alike.
