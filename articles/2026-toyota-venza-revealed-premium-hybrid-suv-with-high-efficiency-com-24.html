What Makes the 2026 Toyota Venza Unique?

The 2026 Toyota Venza is built on the Toyota New Global Architecture (TNGA) platform, which provides a solid foundation for its exceptional handling and ride quality. With its 2.5-liter 4-cylinder engine and electric motor, this vehicle is capable of producing 219 horsepower and 163 lb-ft of torque. But what really sets the 2026 Toyota Venza apart is its advanced safety features, including adaptive cruise control and lane departure warning.

According to Toyota's Chief Engineer, Yoshiki Konishi, "The 2026 Toyota Venza is designed to provide a unique blend of style, comfort, and sustainability. With its hybrid powertrain and advanced safety features, this vehicle is perfect for those who want to reduce their carbon footprint without sacrificing performance or luxury." The 2026 Toyota Venza is available in three trim levels: LE, XLE, and Limited, each with its own unique features and options.

Trim Levels and Pricing

The 2026 Toyota Venza LE starts at $41,875 and comes standard with 18-inch alloy wheels, LED headlights, and Toyota's Entune 3.0 infotainment system. The 2026 Toyota Venza XLE starts at $43,875 and adds 19-inch alloy wheels, heated front seats, and a power liftgate. The 2026 Toyota Venza Limited starts at $47,875 and comes standard with 20-inch alloy wheels, premium leather seats, and a 12.3-inch touchscreen display.
