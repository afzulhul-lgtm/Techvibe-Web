Performance and Handling

Under the hood, the 2026 Mercedes-AMG GLE 63 S Coupe is powered by a 4.0-liter twin-turbo V8 engine, which produces 603 horsepower and 627 lb-ft of torque. This powerful engine is paired with a 9-speed automatic transmission and a rear-biased all-wheel-drive system, making it capable of accelerating from 0-60mph in just 3.8 seconds. What changed? The GLE 63 S's engine and transmission have been carefully tuned to provide a smooth, responsive driving experience, with quick acceleration and precise handling.

The GLE 63 S also features a range of advanced handling and suspension technologies, including adaptive damping and active roll stabilization. These features work together to provide a smooth, comfortable ride, even on rough roads, while also delivering exceptional handling and agility. That's where it gets complicated, as the GLE 63 S's advanced technologies and complex systems require careful calibration and tuning to deliver optimal performance.
