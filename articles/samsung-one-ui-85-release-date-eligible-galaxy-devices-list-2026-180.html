Samsung One UI 8.5 Release Date & Eligible Galaxy Devices List 2026

By {{AUTHOR_NAME}} Senior Editor
Published: May 08, 2026 Updated: May 08, 2026 6 Min Read

May 6, 2026, marks a significant date for Samsung users as the company officially starts rolling out the One UI 8.5 update to its range of Galaxy devices. This update, built on top of Android 16, promises to bring a plethora of new features, performance enhancements, and design changes to eligible devices. The rollout begins in Korea, with other regions such as North America, Europe, India, and more scheduled to receive the update starting May 11, 2026.

The significance of this update cannot be overstated, especially for those invested in the Samsung ecosystem. One UI 8.5 not only brings compatibility with the latest Android version but also introduces exclusive AI features that were first seen in the Galaxy S26 series. These features include Audio Eraser for advanced sound isolation, Creative Studio for AI-driven art generation, Call Screening with real-time transcription capabilities, and Photo Assist for generative image editing. Such updates are crucial for maintaining user satisfaction and competitiveness in the ever-evolving smartphone market.

Samsung One UI 8.5 Official Release Timeline

The official release timeline for One UI 8.5 has been set, with the first wave of updates hitting devices starting May 6, 2026. This initial rollout focuses on the Korean market, providing users there with the first taste of what One UI 8.5 has to offer. Following closely, on May 11, 2026, the update is slated to expand to other key regions, including North America, Europe, India, and beyond, ensuring a broad and timely distribution of the new software.

This strategic rollout plan underscores Samsung's commitment to its global user base, aiming to minimize wait times and ensure that as many devices as possible are updated in a relatively short window. By prioritizing both its newest flagships and older, still-supported models, Samsung demonstrates a customer-centric approach, recognizing the value of a unified and up-to-date ecosystem.

Complete List of Galaxy Phones Getting One UI 8.5

A total of 25 Galaxy devices have been deemed eligible for the One UI 8.5 update, a list that encompasses a wide range of Samsung's recent and popular models. Flagship series such as the Galaxy S25 and S24 are naturally included, given their position at the forefront of Samsung's lineup. Additionally, foldable devices like the Z Fold7 and Z Fold6, as well as the Z Flip7 and Z Flip6, are part of this update, reflecting Samsung's dedication to its innovative foldable form factors.

Furthermore, the update extends to the Tab S11 and S10 series, highlighting Samsung's effort to keep its tablet offerings in line with its smartphones in terms of software and features. This comprehensive approach to updating its devices helps in maintaining a cohesive user experience across different product categories, a key factor for those who utilize multiple Samsung devices as part of their daily routine.

Galaxy S Series Update Schedule

For users of the Galaxy S series, particularly those with the S25 and S24 models, the One UI 8.5 update brings the latest in AI-driven technology and user interface enhancements. Given their status as flagship devices, these models are among the first in line to receive the update, ensuring that they continue to offer the most current and advanced Samsung experience available.

The update schedule for the Galaxy S series, like other models, is staggered, with the rollout beginning in Korea and then expanding globally. This phased approach allows Samsung to monitor the update's performance and address any issues that may arise before widening the rollout to a larger user base.

Exclusive AI Features in One UI 8.5

One of the most exciting aspects of the One UI 8.5 update is the inclusion of exclusive AI features that were initially introduced with the Galaxy S26 series. These features, designed to enhance user productivity and creativity, include Audio Eraser, Creative Studio, Call Screening, and Photo Assist. Audio Eraser utilizes AI to isolate and remove unwanted sounds from audio recordings, offering a powerful tool for content creators and everyday users alike.

Creative Studio, on the other hand, leverages AI to generate artistic content, providing users with a unique platform for expressing their creativity. Call Screening with real-time transcription offers a convenient and accessible way to manage calls, especially for those who prefer visual communication or need to keep records of their conversations. Lastly, Photo Assist uses generative AI to edit and enhance photographs, giving users professional-grade image editing capabilities directly on their Galaxy devices.

Key Takeaways

  • Samsung's One UI 8.5 update is based on Android 16 and starts rolling out on May 6, 2026.
  • 25 Galaxy devices are eligible for the update, including the Galaxy S25, S24, Z Fold7, Z Fold6, Z Flip7, Z Flip6, Tab S11, and Tab S10 series.
  • Exclusive AI features introduced in the update include Audio Eraser, Creative Studio, Call Screening, and Photo Assist.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: When is Samsung One UI 8.5 releasing?

A: Samsung officially started rolling out One UI 8.5 on May 6, 2026, with global expansion on May 11, 2026.

Conclusion

The release of One UI 8.5 marks an important milestone for Samsung, as it not only brings the latest Android version to its devices but also introduces a suite of innovative AI features. The strategic rollout plan, which initially focuses on the Korean market before expanding globally, demonstrates Samsung's commitment to providing its users with the latest technology in a timely and efficient manner. For those with eligible devices, the update promises to enhance their smartphone experience, offering advanced tools for productivity, creativity, and everyday use.

As the smartphone landscape continues to evolve, updates like One UI 8.5 play a crucial role in maintaining user satisfaction and driving innovation. With its blend of new features, performance improvements, and design enhancements, One UI 8.5 is set to keep Samsung at the forefront of the mobile technology sector. Whether you're a loyal Samsung user or considering joining the ecosystem, the One UI 8.5 update is certainly worth looking forward to, offering a glimpse into the exciting possibilities that the future of smartphones holds.
