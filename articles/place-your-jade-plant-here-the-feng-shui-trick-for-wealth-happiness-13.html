Place Your Jade Plant Here: The Feng Shui Trick for Wealth & Happiness

By Sarah Mitchell Senior Tech Editor
Published: May 03, 2026 Updated: May 03, 2026 5 Min Read

A growing number of people are turning to an ancient practice to improve their lives: strategically placing a jade plant in their home or office to attract wealth and happiness.

This trend is rooted in the principles of Feng Shui, an ancient Chinese philosophy that seeks to balance and harmonize the energies in a given space, and it's gaining popularity as people look for simple, non-invasive ways to enhance their well-being.

The Basics of Feng Shui and Jade Plants

Feng Shui is based on the idea that everything in the universe is connected and that by arranging objects in a specific way, one can influence the flow of energy, or "chi," in a space, thereby improving health, wealth, and happiness.

Jade plants, with their small, round leaves and sturdy stems, are particularly well-suited for this purpose, as they are believed to symbolize good luck and prosperity, and are relatively easy to care for, making them a popular choice for people looking to try out Feng Shui.

But what makes jade plants so special, and how can they be used to improve one's life?

Choosing the Right Location

According to Feng Shui principles, the location of a jade plant can have a significant impact on its effectiveness, with different areas of a home or office corresponding to different aspects of life, such as wealth, relationships, and career.

For example, placing a jade plant in the southeast corner of a room, which is believed to be the wealth area, can help attract financial success and prosperity, while placing it in the east, which is associated with family and health, can promote harmony and well-being.

But that's not the whole picture: the direction a jade plant faces can also be important, with some practitioners believing that it should face the door to attract positive energy, while others recommend placing it in a spot where it can be seen from the entrance to the room.

Caring for Your Jade Plant

While the placement of a jade plant is important, it's also crucial to care for it properly, as a healthy, thriving plant is believed to be more effective at attracting positive energy than a sickly or dying one.

This means providing it with the right amount of water, sunlight, and nutrients, as well as pruning it regularly to keep it looking its best, and protecting it from pests and diseases.

The numbers tell a different story, however, as many people who try to use Feng Shui to improve their lives often overlook the importance of proper plant care, which can lead to disappointing results and a lack of faith in the practice.

Key Takeaways

  • Placing a jade plant in the right location can help attract wealth and happiness
  • Proper care and maintenance are crucial for a jade plant to be effective
  • Feng Shui is a complex practice that requires patience, dedication, and an open mind

Common Mistakes to Avoid

While using a jade plant to improve one's life can be a simple and effective way to try out Feng Shui, there are several common mistakes that people make, such as placing the plant in a spot that is too dark or too cold, or forgetting to water it regularly.

Here are a few things to keep in mind when using a jade plant for Feng Shui:

  • Choose a spot with bright, indirect light
  • Water the plant sparingly, as it prefers dry soil
  • Prune the plant regularly to keep it looking its best

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best location for a jade plant?

The best location for a jade plant depends on the specific goals and intentions of the person using it, but common locations include the southeast corner of a room, which is believed to be the wealth area, and the east, which is associated with family and health.

How often should I water my jade plant?

Jade plants prefer dry soil, so it's best to water them sparingly, allowing the soil to dry out completely between waterings.

Can I use any type of plant for Feng Shui?

While any type of plant can be used for Feng Shui, jade plants are particularly well-suited due to their small, round leaves and sturdy stems, which are believed to symbolize good luck and prosperity.

How long does it take to see results from using a jade plant for Feng Shui?

The amount of time it takes to see results from using a jade plant for Feng Shui can vary depending on a number of factors, including the location of the plant, the care and maintenance it receives, and the intentions and energy of the person using it.

Conclusion

Using a jade plant to improve one's life is a simple and effective way to try out Feng Shui, but it's not a magic solution, and it requires patience, dedication, and an open mind to see results.

By choosing the right location, caring for the plant properly, and avoiding common mistakes, anyone can use a jade plant to attract wealth and happiness into their life, and experience the many benefits that Feng Shui has to offer.
