What are the Key Features of the 2026 Tata Harrier?

The 2026 Tata Harrier is packed with a range of luxury features that make it an attractive option for those in the market for a high-end SUV. Some of the key features of the vehicle include ventilated seats, a 10.25-inch touchscreen display, and a 7-inch digital instrument cluster. The Harrier also features a range of advanced safety features, including electronic stability control, hill descent control, and six airbags.

In terms of performance, the 2026 Tata Harrier is powered by a 2.0-liter turbocharged diesel engine that produces 168 horsepower and 350 Nm of torque. The vehicle also features a 6-speed manual transmission and a 6-speed automatic transmission option. With a fuel tank capacity of 50 liters, the Harrier has a range of up to 1,000 km on a single tank.

The 2026 Tata Harrier also features a range of convenience features, including keyless entry, push-button start, and a reverse camera. The vehicle also comes with a range of infotainment features, including Apple CarPlay, Android Auto, and a 9-speaker JBL sound system.
