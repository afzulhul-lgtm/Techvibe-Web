Design and Features

The new Maruti Suzuki Jimny 5-door mini SUV features a bold and rugged design, with a distinctive front grille, swept-back headlights, and a sloping roofline. The interior is equally impressive, with a spacious and well-appointed cabin that offers ample legroom and cargo space. The dashboard is equipped with a range of modern features, including a touchscreen infotainment system, climate control, and a multifunction steering wheel. The vehicle also comes with a range of safety features, such as airbags, anti-lock braking, and electronic stability control.

One of the key highlights of the Maruti Suzuki Jimny is its off-road capability, which is made possible by its four-wheel-drive system and high ground clearance. The vehicle is also equipped with a range of advanced technologies, including hill descent control and a rear differential lock, which provide added traction and control on challenging terrain. Additionally, the Jimny features a range of convenience features, such as a rearview camera, parking sensors, and a smart keyless entry system.

In terms of engine options, the Maruti Suzuki Jimny is expected to be powered by a range of petrol and diesel engines, including a 1.5-liter petrol unit and a 1.3-liter diesel unit. The vehicle will also feature a range of transmission options, including a 5-speed manual and a 4-speed automatic.

Technical Specifications

The Maruti Suzuki Jimny 5-door mini SUV is built on a ladder-frame chassis, which provides a solid foundation for its off-road capabilities. The vehicle features a range of technical specifications, including a wheelbase of 2,590mm, a length of 3,645mm, and a width of 1,645mm. The Jimny also features a range of suspension components, including coil springs and shock absorbers, which provide a smooth and comfortable ride on both on-road and off-road terrain.
