From what's been reported so far, the new motorhome is set to feature MBUX (Mercedes-Benz User Experience) infotainment with AI voice control, route planning integrated with the motorhome's systems, and smart home integration for lighting, climate, and security.

But why does this matter? The answer lies in the level of luxury and technology that the 2026 Mercedes Motorhome Unveiled is bringing to the table. That's where it gets interesting - the integration of MBUX and autonomous driving assistance systems is seen as the key differentiator, with future models potentially offering Level 2+ automated highway driving.
