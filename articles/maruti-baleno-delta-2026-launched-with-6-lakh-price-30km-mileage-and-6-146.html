What are the Key Features of the Maruti Baleno Delta 2026?

The Maruti Baleno Delta 2026 is equipped with a range of features that make it an attractive option for buyers. Some of the notable features include 15-inch alloy wheels, LED projector headlights, and a 7-inch touchscreen infotainment system. Additionally, the vehicle comes with dual airbags, side airbags, and curtain airbags, providing comprehensive protection for occupants. But what about the vehicle's performance, and how does it handle on the road?

According to experts, the Maruti Baleno Delta 2026 is a well-rounded vehicle that offers a smooth driving experience. The 1.2-liter petrol engine provides adequate power, while the 5-speed manual transmission ensures seamless gear shifts. The vehicle's ground clearance of 170mm also makes it suitable for navigating rough roads and potholes.
