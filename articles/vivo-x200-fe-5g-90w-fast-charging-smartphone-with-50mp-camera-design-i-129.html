Vivo X200 FE 5G 90W fast charging smartphone with 50MP camera, design is stylish

By Sarah Mitchell
Published: March 04, 2026 Updated: March 04, 2026 5 Min Read

Get ready to experience the future of smartphones with the Vivo X200 FE 5G, a powerhouse of a device that packs a punch in a sleek and stylish package. Announced in June 2025 and available in the market since July, this phone is a game-changer in the Indian market, offering flagship-level performance without the bulk.

With its 6.31-inch LTPO AMOLED display, 120Hz refresh rate, and 1.5K resolution, this phone is perfect for binge-watching your favorite shows or editing photos on the go. The 90.2% screen-to-body ratio makes for an immersive experience, and the 5000 nits peak brightness ensures that you can use your phone even in the brightest of environments.

Design and Display

The Vivo X200 FE 5G boasts a sleek and stylish design, with a glass front and back, and an aluminum frame that screams luxury. The phone is available in four colors - Luxe Grey, Frost Blue, Amber Yellow, and Pink Vibe - each of which pops under sunlight, making it a style statement. The aerospace-grade metal frame and Schott Xensation Core glass add toughness to the device without adding heft, and the IP68/IP69 ratings ensure that it can withstand dust, splashes, and even high-pressure jets.

Camera Capabilities

The Vivo X200 FE 5G features a triple rear camera setup, which includes a 50MP main camera, a 50MP periscope telephoto lens, and an 8MP ultrawide lens. The camera is equipped with features such as laser AF, T* coating, and multifocal portraits, which enable it to capture stunning images in various lighting conditions. The phone also features a 50MP selfie camera, which is perfect for taking selfies and making video calls.

According to a recent report by Counterpoint Research, the Vivo X200 FE 5G is one of the top-selling 5G smartphones in the Indian market, thanks to its impressive camera capabilities and sleek design.

Performance and Battery Life

The Vivo X200 FE 5G is powered by the MediaTek Dimensity 9300+ processor, which provides a seamless and efficient performance. The phone also features a large 6500mAh battery, which provides up to 58 hours of battery life, even with heavy usage. The 90W FlashCharge technology enables the phone to charge quickly, reaching 100% in just 57 minutes.

Pricing and Availability

The Vivo X200 FE 5G is available in two variants - 12GB/256GB and 16GB/512GB - which are priced at ₹54,999 and ₹59,999, respectively. The phone is available for purchase on various online platforms, including Flipkart, Amazon, and Vivo's official website. Customers can also avail of various launch offers, such as 10% cashback and no-cost EMI options.
