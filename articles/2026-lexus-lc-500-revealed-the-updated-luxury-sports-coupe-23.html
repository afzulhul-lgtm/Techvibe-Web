2026 Lexus LC 500 Revealed: The Updated Luxury Sports Coupe

By {{AUTHOR_NAME}} Senior Editor
Published: May 08, 2026 Updated: May 08, 2026 5 Min Read

The 2026 Lexus LC 500 is making waves with its updated luxury sports coupe, featuring a powerful 5.0-litre naturally aspirated V8 engine that produces 471 horsepower and has a 7,100 rpm redline. This distinctive engine delivers an exhaust note unlike any other in the luxury coupe segment, providing a genuine, uncompressed V8 chorus that synthetic sound systems cannot replicate. With its updated 10-speed automatic transmission, the LC 500's shift speed has improved by 15 percent in Sport+ mode, while maintaining a relaxed character in Comfort mode.

The significance of the 2026 Lexus LC 500's updates cannot be overstated, as they sharpen the vehicle's driving dynamics and modernize its technology suite. This is particularly important in the current luxury sports coupe market, where competition is fierce and manufacturers are constantly striving to outdo one another. The LC 500's unique design, which has made it one of the most photographed cars on the road, has been preserved, while its performance and features have been enhanced to meet the demands of discerning drivers.

Key Specs and Features Breakdown

The 2026 Lexus LC 500's key specs include its 5.0-litre V8 engine, which produces 471 horsepower, and its 10-speed automatic transmission. The vehicle's suspension setup has been updated with revised damper tuning, improving body control in aggressive driving while preserving the GT car ride comfort that defines the LC's character. Additionally, the LC 500 features updated LED lighting signatures front and rear, new 21-inch wheel designs in dark and light finishes, and two new body color options.

The interior of the 2026 Lexus LC 500 has also been updated, with a new 12.3-inch touchscreen infotainment system replacing the divisive trackpad interface from previous models. This change has been requested by enthusiasts for years and is a significant improvement. The vehicle also features premium Mark Levinson 13-speaker audio and available semi-aniline leather with hand-pleated panels, maintaining the interior's prestige.

In terms of pricing, the 2026 Lexus LC 500 starts at approximately $98,000, while the LC 500 Convertible commands a premium starting above $100,000. These prices reflect the vehicle's luxury features and performance capabilities, making it a compelling option for those seeking a high-end sports coupe.

How It Compares to the Previous LC 500

The 2026 Lexus LC 500's updates are significant when compared to the previous model. The revised damper tuning and updated suspension setup improve the vehicle's handling and ride comfort, making it a more refined and enjoyable driving experience. The new 12.3-inch touchscreen infotainment system is also a major improvement, providing a more intuitive and user-friendly interface.

In terms of performance, the 2026 Lexus LC 500's 5.0-litre V8 engine remains unchanged, but the updated 10-speed automatic transmission provides faster shift speeds and improved responsiveness. This makes the vehicle more engaging and enjoyable to drive, particularly in Sport+ mode.
