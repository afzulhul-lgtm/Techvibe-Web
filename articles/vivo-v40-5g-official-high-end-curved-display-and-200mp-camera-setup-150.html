Key Features

The Vivo V40 5G comes with a range of exciting features, including a large 6.7-inch curved display, a powerful Qualcomm Snapdragon 888 processor, and a massive 4500mAh battery. The device also supports fast charging, with the ability to charge up to 80% in just 30 minutes.

Design and Display

The Vivo V40 5G boasts a stunning curved display with a resolution of 1080 x 2376 pixels. The device features a sleek and premium design, with a glass back and a metal frame. The curved display provides an immersive viewing experience, making it perfect for watching videos, playing games, and browsing the web.

Camera and Performance

The Vivo V40 5G features a quad-camera setup, with a 200MP primary sensor, a 12MP front camera, and a range of AI-powered features. The device is powered by a Qualcomm Snapdragon 888 processor, paired with up to 12GB of RAM and 256GB of internal storage. The Vivo V40 5G also supports 5G connectivity, providing fast and seamless data speeds.
