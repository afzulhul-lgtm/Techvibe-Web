Key Details and What They Mean

One of the standout features of the Vivo V40 5G is its 200MP camera setup, which includes a primary sensor, a telephoto lens, and an ultra-wide-angle lens, providing users with a versatile photography experience, and the device's advanced image processing capabilities, which enable features such as portrait mode, night mode, and super macro mode.

The device's high-end curved display is another key feature, providing an immersive viewing experience with vibrant colors and crisp details, and the device's powerful performance, courtesy of its advanced processor and ample storage, makes it well-suited for demanding tasks such as gaming and video editing.

Here are some key specifications of the Vivo V40 5G:

  • 6.7-inch high-end curved display with a 120Hz refresh rate
  • 200MP primary sensor with optical zoom and portrait mode
  • Large battery with fast charging capabilities
  • Advanced processor with ample storage and RAM
