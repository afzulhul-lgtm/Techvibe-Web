Introduction to Porsche Design Magic 7

The Porsche Design Magic 7 is the latest addition to the luxury smartphone market, offering a unique blend of style, performance, and innovation. According to

Dr. Jan Becker, CEO of Porsche Design, "The Magic 7 is designed to provide an unparalleled mobile experience, combining the latest technology with premium materials and sleek design."
With its focus on luxury and performance, the Magic 7 is set to appeal to a wide range of users, from business professionals to tech enthusiasts.

Design and Display

The Magic 7 features a stunning 6.7-inch AMOLED display, with a high-resolution screen and a 120Hz refresh rate. The device is crafted from premium materials, including titanium and glass, providing a sleek and durable design. The phone's dimensions are 162.8 x 74.9 x 8.5mm, making it comfortable to hold and use. The Magic 7 is available in three colors: black, silver, and gold.

Performance and Camera

The Magic 7 is powered by a large 5000mAh battery and features a fast charging system, allowing users to quickly top up their device. The phone is equipped with a Qualcomm Snapdragon 8 Gen 2 chipset, providing fast performance and efficient battery life. The Magic 7 also features a quad-camera setup, including a 50MP primary sensor, a 12MP front camera, and support for 8K video recording.

Camera Specifications

The Magic 7's camera system is designed to provide professional-grade image quality, with advanced features such as optical zoom, portrait mode, and low-light enhancement. The phone's camera app is intuitive and easy to use, allowing users to capture stunning photos and videos with ease.

Battery Life and Charging

The Magic 7 features a long-lasting 5000mAh battery, providing up to 12 hours of internet browsing, 18 hours of video playback, and 25 hours of talk time. The phone also supports fast charging, allowing users to quickly top up their device. The Magic 7 comes with a 65W charger, providing fast and efficient charging.

Whether you're a tech enthusiast or a luxury connoisseur, the Magic 7 is the ultimate choice for anyone looking for a premium mobile experience.
