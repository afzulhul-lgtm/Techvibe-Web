iQOO Most Powerful Snapdragon 8 Gen 5 Processor 5G Phone Launched, Best for Gaming with 16GB RAM

By Sarah Mitchell
Published: March 09, 2026 Updated: March 09, 2026 5 Min Read

The wait is over, and the most powerful smartphone of 2026 is finally here. iQOO has launched its flagship device, the iQOO 15R, which boasts the latest Snapdragon 8 Gen 5 processor, 16GB RAM, and a stunning 144Hz display. This phone is specifically designed for gaming enthusiasts, offering an unparalleled mobile gaming experience.

Introduction to iQOO 15R

The iQOO 15R is the latest addition to the iQOO family, and it's packed with features that make it a game-changer in the smartphone industry. With its powerful processor, ample RAM, and high-refresh-rate display, this phone is perfect for playing graphics-intensive games and multitasking.

The iQOO 15R is available in two stunning colors: Dark Knight and Triumph Silver, both of which exude a premium feel. The phone also features an IP68 rating, making it resistant to dust and water.

Key Features and Specifications

The iQOO 15R is powered by the Qualcomm Snapdragon 8 Gen 5 processor, which is the fastest and most efficient processor available in the market. The phone also features 16GB of LPDDR5X RAM, which provides lightning-fast performance and seamless multitasking.

The iQOO 15R has a large 6.78-inch AMOLED display with a 144Hz refresh rate, making it perfect for gaming and watching videos. The phone also features a massive 7600mAh battery with 100W fast charging, which can charge the battery from 0 to 100% in just 30 minutes.

Camera Specifications

The iQOO 15R features a quad-camera setup with a 50MP Sony LYT-700V primary sensor, a 12MP front camera, and support for 8K video recording. The phone also features advanced camera software with features like optical zoom, portrait mode, and night mode.

Gaming Performance

The iQOO 15R is designed specifically for gaming, and it delivers exceptional performance. The phone features a large cooling system, which keeps the device cool even during intense gaming sessions. The phone also supports 144 FPS gaming, making it perfect for playing fast-paced games.

The iQOO 15R has achieved an impressive Antutu score of 1,200,000+, which is one of the highest scores in the industry. The phone also features advanced AI-powered features like AI-powered game optimization and AI-powered cooling.

Price and Availability

The iQOO 15R is priced at ₹49,990 for the base variant, which features 8GB of RAM and 128GB of internal storage. The top-end variant, which features 16GB of RAM and 256GB of internal storage, is priced at ₹59,990.

The phone is available for pre-order on Amazon and the iQOO official website, with sales starting from March 15, 2026. Customers who pre-order the phone will receive a free pair of TWS earbuds and a ₹2,000 discount on the phone.

Comparison with Other Phones

The iQOO 15R is compared to other flagship phones like the OnePlus 15R and the Samsung Galaxy S23. The phone offers better performance, a larger battery, and a higher-refresh-rate display than its competitors.

The iQOO 15R also features advanced software with OriginOS 6, which is based on Android 16. The phone receives regular software updates, ensuring that it stays secure and features the latest advancements in technology.
