Introduction to Mountain Climbing Accidents

Mountain climbing is a popular adventure sport that attracts thousands of enthusiasts every year. However, it can also be a deadly activity, with many climbers losing their lives due to accidents and injuries. According to statistics, the death rate for mountain climbers is higher than for any other adventure sport. The main causes of mountain climbing accidents include falls, avalanches, and inclement weather.

Mountain rescue teams play a crucial role in saving the lives of climbers who are injured or stranded in the mountains.
