Introduction to the Mahindra Scorpio Classic

The Mahindra Scorpio Classic is more than just a vehicle; it's a symbol of India's unyielding spirit. With its rugged design and powerful 2.2-litre diesel engine, it has been a favorite among SUV enthusiasts for decades. As of March 3, 2026, the Scorpio Classic continues to dominate the Indian roads with its impressive features and capabilities.

Key Takeaways

- The Mahindra Scorpio Classic comes with a 2.2-litre diesel engine, producing 132hp and 300Nm of torque. - It features a 6-speed manual transmission and rear-wheel drive. - The SUV has a seating capacity of up to 9 passengers and offers ample cargo space. - The Scorpio Classic has a 5-star Global NCAP rating, ensuring safety on the road.

Design and Features

The Mahindra Scorpio Classic boasts a fabulous design that exudes ruggedness and authority. Its boxy silhouette, chrome accents, and signature vertical slats make it a head-turner on the road. The SUV is available in various color options, including Stealth Black and Everest White, which add to its dramatic appeal.

Interior Features

The Scorpio Classic's interior is simple, sturdy, and satisfying. The S11 trim comes with a 9-inch touchscreen, Android Auto/Apple CarPlay, auto AC with rear vents, and a decent speaker setup. The SUV offers generous space for passengers and cargo, making it ideal for family duties or rough use.

Performance and Capabilities

The Mahindra Scorpio Classic is powered by a 2.2-litre mHawk diesel engine that produces 132hp and 300Nm of torque. Paired with a 6-speed manual transmission and rear-wheel drive, the SUV delivers impressive performance and capabilities. Its ladder-frame construction and high ground clearance make it perfect for off-road adventures.

Suspension and Handling

The Scorpio Classic's suspension is tuned firm, providing a smooth ride on rough roads. Its high perch and excellent visibility make it easy to navigate through city chaos. The SUV's steering is responsive, and its braking system is reliable, ensuring a safe and comfortable driving experience.

Safety and Reliability

The Mahindra Scorpio Classic has a 5-star Global NCAP rating, ensuring safety on the road. Its ladder-frame construction, dual airbags, and ABS provide excellent protection for passengers. The SUV's reliability is also commendable, with many owners praising its low maintenance costs and long-term durability.

Comparison with Rivals

The Mahindra Scorpio Classic competes with rivals like the Hyundai Creta and Mahindra Thar Roxx. While these SUVs offer urban polish and modern features, the Scorpio Classic stands out with its value and versatility. Its towing capacity, seating flexibility, and off-road prowess make it a better choice for those who need a rugged and reliable SUV.

What is the price of the Mahindra Scorpio Classic?

The Mahindra Scorpio Classic starts at Rs 13 lakh ex-showroom and goes up to Rs 16.90 lakh for the top-end S11 trim.

What are the key features of the Mahindra Scorpio Classic?

The Mahindra Scorpio Classic comes with a 2.2-litre diesel engine, 6-speed manual transmission, rear-wheel drive, and a range of interior features like a 9-inch touchscreen and auto AC.

Is the Mahindra Scorpio Classic safe?

Yes, the Mahindra Scorpio Classic has a 5-star Global NCAP rating, ensuring safety on the road. It also features dual airbags, ABS, and a ladder-frame construction for added protection.
