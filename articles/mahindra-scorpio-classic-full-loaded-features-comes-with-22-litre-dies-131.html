Frequently Asked Questions

What is the fuel efficiency of the Mahindra Scorpio Classic?

The Mahindra Scorpio Classic has a fuel efficiency of 12 kmpl in the city and 15 kmpl on the highway. However, this may vary depending on driving conditions and other factors.

What are the safety features of the Mahindra Scorpio Classic?

The Mahindra Scorpio Classic comes with a range of safety features, including dual airbags, ABS with EBD, and rear parking sensors. Additionally, the car has a 5-star safety rating from the Global NCAP.

What is the price of the Mahindra Scorpio Classic?

The price of the Mahindra Scorpio Classic starts at $25,000 and goes up to $35,000, depending on the variant and features. However, prices may vary depending on the location and other factors.

What are the variants of the Mahindra Scorpio Classic?

The Mahindra Scorpio Classic is available in several variants, including the S3, S5, S7, and S11. Each variant comes with its own set of features and specs, so it's worth doing some research to find the one that's right for you.

What is the warranty period of the Mahindra Scorpio Classic?

The Mahindra Scorpio Classic comes with a 3-year/100,000 km warranty, which covers parts and labor. Additionally, the car has a 6-year anti-perforation warranty and a 3-year paint warranty.
