What Makes the Maruti Ciaz a Luxury Design Sedan?

The Maruti Ciaz is designed to provide a luxurious driving experience, with its sleek and sophisticated exterior design, premium interior features, and advanced safety features. The sedan boasts a 1.5-liter petrol engine that produces 103.26bhp of power and 138Nm of torque, making it a powerful and efficient vehicle. Additionally, the Maruti Ciaz features a 5-speed manual transmission and a 4-speed automatic transmission option, giving drivers the flexibility to choose their preferred driving mode.

Inside the Maruti Ciaz, you'll find a range of premium features, including leather upholstery, wooden trim, and a 7-inch touchscreen infotainment system. The sedan also features a range of advanced safety features, including ABS with EBD, dual airbags, and rear parking sensors. With its unique blend of style, comfort, and technology, the Maruti Ciaz is an attractive option for car buyers looking for a luxury design sedan.

According to Rajesh Singh, Senior Manager at Maruti Suzuki, "The Maruti Ciaz is designed to provide a luxurious driving experience, with its sleek and sophisticated exterior design, premium interior features, and advanced safety features. We believe that the Maruti Ciaz is an attractive option for car buyers looking for a luxury design sedan that offers a unique blend of style, comfort, and technology."
