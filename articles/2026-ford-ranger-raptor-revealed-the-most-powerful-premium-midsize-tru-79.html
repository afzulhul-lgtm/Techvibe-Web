What Makes the 2026 Ford Ranger Raptor So Powerful?

The 2026 Ford Ranger Raptor is built on a high-strength steel frame, providing a solid foundation for its impressive powertrain. The 3.0-liter EcoBoost engine is paired with a 10-speed automatic transmission, allowing for seamless shifting and optimal performance. But what about the suspension and handling? The Ranger Raptor features a 2.0-inch live valve suspension system, which provides improved stability and control on both on- and off-road terrain.

But power is not the only aspect of this midsize truck. The 2026 Ford Ranger Raptor also features a 12-inch touchscreen display with SYNC 4 infotainment system, providing an intuitive and connected driving experience. With Apple CarPlay and Android Auto compatibility, you can stay connected on the go. And with a 360-degree camera system, you'll have a clear view of your surroundings, no matter where you are.
