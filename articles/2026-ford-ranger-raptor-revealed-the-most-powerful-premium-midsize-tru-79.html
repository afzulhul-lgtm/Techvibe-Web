Introduction to the 2026 Ford Ranger Raptor

The 2026 Ford Ranger Raptor is the latest addition to Ford's lineup of 4x4 trucks. This midsize truck is designed to deliver exceptional performance, both on and off the road. With its powerful engine and advanced features, the 2026 Ford Ranger Raptor is set to dominate the midsize truck market.

Engine and Performance

The 2026 Ford Ranger Raptor is powered by a 3.0L EcoBoost V6 engine, producing 392 horsepower and 430 lb.-ft. of torque. This engine is paired with a 10-speed automatic transmission and features a two-speed transfer case for exceptional off-road performance.
