2026 Range Rover Motorhome Unveiled: The Ultimate Luxury Ride with Built-In Bedrooms

By Mike Reynolds Senior Editor
Published: May 10, 2026 Updated: May 10, 2026 6 Min Read

The 2026 Range Rover Motorhome is a game-changer in the world of luxury vehicles, combining Land Rover's renowned DNA with a bespoke coach conversion. This niche segment has been gaining popularity, with custom coachbuilders in the UK and Europe developing Range Rover-based expedition motorhomes for years. The extended-wheelbase versions of the full-size Range Rover serve as the donor platform, featuring a 3.0-liter inline-six mild-hybrid engine that produces 395 horsepower and 406 lb-ft of torque.

But why does this matter? The answer lies in the unique blend of luxury and off-road capability that the 2026 Range Rover Motorhome offers. With the Terrain Response 2 system, this vehicle can traverse terrain that conventional motorhomes can only dream of. And, with interior configurations that include a fold-flat sleeping platform for two, a compact kitchen with induction hob, and a bathroom with wet room, it's the perfect vehicle for long-range expeditions.

Key Specs and Features Breakdown

The 2026 Range Rover Motorhome's interior is compact, with a total sleeping and living space of under 100 square feet. However, this is more than made up for by the vehicle's unmatched off-road capability. The engine, which is paired with the Terrain Response 2 system, allows the vehicle to tackle challenging terrain with ease. From what's been reported so far, the 2026 Range Rover Motorhome's mpg is not yet available, but it's expected to be comparable to other vehicles in its class.

That's where it gets interesting. Companies such as Project Kahn in the UK and several German coachbuilders have created notable examples of bespoke Range Rover motorhomes that have attracted significant attention at overland and adventure vehicle shows in 2024 and 2025. These custom conversions start around £250,000 and can exceed £400,000, making them a significant investment for those who want the ultimate luxury ride.

How It Compares to the Previous Model

So, how does the 2026 Range Rover Motorhome compare to its predecessor? While there isn't much information available on the previous model, it's clear that the 2026 Range Rover Motorhome is a significant improvement. The new model features a more powerful engine, improved off-road capability, and a more luxurious interior. Yet, it's hard to say exactly why the previous model didn't quite live up to expectations, but it's clear that the 2026 Range Rover Motorhome is a major step forward.

What that means, practically speaking, is that the 2026 Range Rover Motorhome is a serious contender in the luxury vehicle market. With its unique blend of off-road capability and luxury features, it's the perfect vehicle for those who want to explore the great outdoors in style. And, with a price tag to match, it's clear that this vehicle is aimed at a very specific market.
