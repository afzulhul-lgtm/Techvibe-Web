Design and Features

The 2026 Mitsubishi L200 boasts a bold new design, with a striking Dynamic Shield grille and T-shaped LED headlights. The flared fenders and 18-inch alloys add to the vehicle's rugged and aggressive stance. Inside, the reworked dash features soft-touch materials and tough plastics, while the 9-inch touchscreen and wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto provide a seamless and connected driving experience.

Off-road Capabilities

The 2026 Mitsubishi L200 is designed to tackle even the most challenging terrain, with its Super All-Wheel Control 4WD-II system and low-range gearing. The vehicle also features a rear diff lock, mud, gravel, and snow modes, and an impressive ground clearance of 8.1 inches. Whether you're navigating rocky trails or crossing shallow rivers, the Mitsubishi L200 is equipped to handle it.

Off-road Features

* Super All-Wheel Control 4WD-II system * Low-range gearing * Rear diff lock * Mud, gravel, and snow modes * 8.1 inches of ground clearance

Engine and Performance

The 2026 Mitsubishi L200 is powered by a 2.4-liter turbo diesel engine, producing 201 horsepower and 470 Nm torque. The vehicle also features a six-speed automatic or six-speed manual transmission, and can accelerate from 0-60 mph in just 10 seconds. With its bi-turbo options and ladder-frame chassis, the Mitsubishi L200 is designed to deliver exceptional performance and capability.

Tech and Safety Features

The 2026 Mitsubishi L200 features a range of advanced tech and safety features, including a 9-inch touchscreen, wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto, and a 12.3-inch digital cluster. The vehicle also comes equipped with MI-PILOT Assist, adaptive cruise control, lane-keeping, blind-spot alerts, and a 360-camera view. With its forward collision braking and hill descent control, the Mitsubishi L200 is designed to provide a safe and confident driving experience.

Trim Levels and Options

The 2026 Mitsubishi L200 is available in a range of trim levels, including the base GLX, GLS, Street SE, and Black Edition. Each trim level offers a unique set of features and options, including alloy wheels, cameras, leather seats, and a sunroof. With its spray-in bed liners, five-foot beds, and regular or extended cabs, the Mitsubishi L200 is designed to meet the needs of a variety of drivers and applications.
