What should I do to prepare for the storm?

You should stock up on essential supplies, such as food, water, and medications, and charge your devices in case of a power outage. It's also a good idea to have a backup plan in case you need to evacuate.

How long is the storm expected to last?

The storm is expected to last for several hours, but the exact duration is uncertain. It's essential to stay informed and follow the latest updates from trusted sources to get the most up-to-date information.

What are the potential risks to public health and safety?

The potential risks include hypothermia, frostbite, and injuries from accidents or falls. It's essential to take precautions, such as dressing warmly and avoiding travel, to minimize these risks.

Can I still travel during the storm?

It's highly recommended that you avoid all non-essential travel during the storm. If you must travel, make sure to check the latest road conditions and weather updates before embarking on your journey.
