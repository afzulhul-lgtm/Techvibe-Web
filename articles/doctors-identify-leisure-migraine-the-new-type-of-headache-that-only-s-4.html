What is a Leisure Migraine?

A Leisure Migraine is a type of headache that occurs when an individual is relaxed or not engaged in any strenuous activity. Unlike traditional migraines that are often triggered by stress, certain foods, or environmental factors, this new type of migraine seems to strike when we're letting our guard down. So, what exactly causes a Leisure Migraine?
